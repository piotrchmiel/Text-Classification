Theresa May in France for Calais strike talks - BBC News

2 July 2015 Last updated at 13:41 BST
Home Secretary Theresa May has been meeting her French counterpart in Paris to discuss strikes by ferry workers at the port of Calais.
Thousands of lorry drivers have been stranded on the M20 in Kent after the coast-bound carriageway was shut for 26 miles.
A blockade at Calais which started on Monday has been suspended and limited services resumed last night.
Andrew Plant reports.
