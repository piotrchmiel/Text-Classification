NHS 'lags behind' other countries' health systems - BBC News

3 July 2015 Last updated at 07:03 BST
The NHS lags behind other countries on preventing unnecessary hospital admissions and improving survival rates, a report has suggested.
The review by the Nuffield Trust think tank of 15 wealthy nations found lower cancer survival and higher death rates from heart attacks and strokes.
BBC health correspondent Dominic Hughes reports from a class aiming to improve survival rates.
