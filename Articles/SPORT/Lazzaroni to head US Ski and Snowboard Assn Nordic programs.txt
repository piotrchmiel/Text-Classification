Lazzaroni to head US Ski and Snowboard Assn Nordic programs

PARK CITY, Utah (AP) -- Robert Lazzaroni has been named the Nordic domestic program director for the U.S. Ski and Snowboard Association.
The USSA announced Thursday that Lazzaroni will administer programs involving membership, competition management, rankings, education for judges and officials and other tasks in cross country, ski jumping and Nordic combined.
Lazzaroni most recently managed programs at the Utah Olympic Park.
