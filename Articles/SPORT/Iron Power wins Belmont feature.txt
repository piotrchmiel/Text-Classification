
NEW YORK (AP) -- Iron Power was the 8-1 upset winner of the $85,000 allowance feature Thursday at Belmont Park.
The 5-year-old gray gelding set the pace, prevailing by 1 3-4 lengths for his sixth win in 22 starts. Iron Power, with Jose Ortiz aboard for trainer Christophe Clement, ran the 1 3-8 miles over the firm turf in 2:14.93.
Iron Power paid $18.80, $8.10 and $5.90.
Can'thelpbelieving finished second, only to be disqualified to seventh for interference turning for home.
Reflecting placed second, returning $5.60 and $3.90.
