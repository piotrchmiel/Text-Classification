She's Not Here rallies to win at Del Mar

DEL MAR, Calif. (AP) -- She's Not Here rallied to edge fellow long-shot Beat of The Drum by 1 3/4 lengths Thursday at Del Mar in the $78,000 feature race for fillies and mares.
Ridden by Hall of Famer Mike Smith and trained by Vickie Oliver, the 4-year-old She's Not Here ran a mile on the turf course in 1:34.61 in her California debut. She returned $25.60, $10.60 and $4 for her fourth victory in 15 starts.
Beat of The Drum returned $18.20 and $6, and Indecise paid $3 to show.
