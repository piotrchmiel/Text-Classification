Premature babies cared for in Newcastle's RVI - BBC News

2 July 2015 Last updated at 18:22 BST
Babies weighing under one pound are cared for at one of the UK's busiest maternity ward.
The Special Care Baby Unit at Newcastle's Royal Victoria Infirmary looks after prematurely born babies weighing as little as 500 grammes.
BBC Look North's Sharon Barbour was given exclusive access to see the work they do.
