'Health conscious' push for Scottish seaweed market - BBC News

4 August 2015 Last updated at 16:53 BST
Think of a nutrient-rich snack and seaweed may not be an obvious choice.
One company in Edinburgh hopes to change that by encouraging us to use it as an ingredient in savoury and sweet dishes.
Mara Seaweed has been exporting record levels of Scottish seaweed to Europe and Asia.
BBC Scotland's Lisa Summers has been to see the harvest along the shoreline in the East Neuk.
