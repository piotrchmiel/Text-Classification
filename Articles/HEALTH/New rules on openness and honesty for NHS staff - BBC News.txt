New rules on openness and honesty for NHS staff - BBC News

29 June 2015 Last updated at 07:11 BST
Doctors, nurses and midwives across the UK are being given new guidelines on being honest and open with patients when things go wrong.
The "duty of candour" guidelines make it clear that patients should expect an apology in person, as well as an explanation of what happened.
Dominic Hughes reports.
