A day in the life of a UKIP MEP on a mission in Calais - BBC News

7 August 2015 Last updated at 23:21 BST
UKIP MEP Mike Hookem believes British and French authorities aren't doing nearly enough to stop migrants from coming to the UK.
He decided to head to Calais on a fact-finding mission.
Newsnight's James Clayton and Jack Garland followed him around for a day.
